

# AI Credibility Slab for Landing Page

## Concept
Not a duplicate of the admin tech page — this is a **marketing credibility signal** for AI-savvy visitors. Think "Under the Hood" or "The AI Behind the Brand." It answers: *"Is this real AI or just a wrapper?"* by surfacing just enough technical depth to impress without overwhelming.

## What to Show
- **"Powered by Google Gemini & OpenAI"** — text-only, no logos, with a brief line about multi-model orchestration (not just one model, multiple specialized models for different tasks)
- **Three credibility pillars** as visual cards:
  1. **Multi-Model Orchestration** — "8 specialized AI models selected per task — text generation, image creation, content analysis, voice extraction"
  2. **Content DNA Engine** — "Your brand voice isn't a prompt. It's a living profile built from your actual content, continuously refined"
  3. **Evaluation Loop** — "Every output is scored against your brand platform before you see it. Not vibes — metrics"
- **A single stat strip** reinforcing scale: "8 AI Models · 35+ Cloud Functions · Real-time Brand Scoring"
